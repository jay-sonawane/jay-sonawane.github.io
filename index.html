I am a final year student at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay pursuing a Dual Degree (B.Tech+M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Nanoelectronics and Integrated Ciruits.

Currently, I am involved in research with the MeLoDe- Memory Logic Device and Design group, with Prof. Udayan Ganguly and Prof. Veeresh Deshpande. My Dual Degree thesis is based on BEOL compatible 1T-1R (RRAM and ITO MOSFET) design, fabrication and modeling for 3D monolithic integration. Earlier I worked on modeling of GF32nm SOI MOSFET for application in band-to-band tunneling neuron. I am also working on integration of variability aware degradation in CARAT for SRAM and sense amplifier circuits, under the guidance of Prof. Souvik Mahapatra.



Research Interests

An enduring fascination with nanoscale devices fuels my profound academic passion for nanoelectronics, device physics, and modeling. I am interested in designing and fabrication of novel devices with accurate modeling, for efficient device-to-circuit system level translation.
